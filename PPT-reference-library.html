PFAS Reference Library

Canonical sources for federal, state, international, scientific, and legal PFAS information
← Back to the tracker

This reference library catalogues the canonical sources Library Services uses to build and maintain the PFAS Regulatory & Industry Tracker. The list is divided by category. Each entry includes a short note on what the source covers and when it is most useful.

For time-sensitive items (recent reports, syntheses, and analyses with publication dates), see the Reference Shelf section on the tracker itself.

Federal regulators & agencies

EPA

  • EPA PFAS landing page The federal framing across all media: drinking water, air, soil, biosolids, AFFF, consumer products.
  • EPA NPDWR (Drinking Water) PFAS National Primary Drinking Water Regulation status, MCL values, and rule history.
  • EPA CERCLA & PFAS CERCLA designations, enforcement discretion policy, passive-receiver categories.
  • EPA Effluent Guidelines Program Plan PFAS ELGs for OCPSF, metal finishing, landfill leachate.
  • EPA Federal Register page All EPA rulemaking notices. The tracker's Live FR Stream pulls a PFAS-filtered view of this feed.

FDA & CPSC

  • FDA PFAS in food page Food-contact substances, Total Diet Study, citizen petition responses.
  • Consumer Product Safety Commission PFAS in consumer products; CPSC investigations and recalls.

DoD

  • DoD PFAS Cleanup Program Installation Restoration Program, PFAS investigation and remediation status across DoD bases.
  • DENIX (DoD Environment, Safety & Occupational Health Network) Technical guidance, policy documents, and program updates for DoD environmental work.

HHS / NIEHS

  • NIEHS PFAS research portal Peer-reviewed science aggregator covering toxicology, epidemiology, and exposure science.
  • ATSDR PFAS Toxicological profiles, public-health assessments, exposure investigations.

State regulators

The tracker monitors 19 state PFAS programs. Direct links to each state's PFAS landing page appear on the individual state cards in the State Watchlist section. The list below highlights the states with the most active programs.

  • California OEHHA — PFAS
  • New York DEC — PFAS
  • New Jersey DEP — PFAS
  • Massachusetts MassDEP — PFAS

  • Washington Ecology — PFAS

International regulators

  • ECHA — PFAS (EU) REACH restriction proposal, RAC and SEAC opinions, Annex XVII status.
  • UK HSE — PFAS REACH UK REACH PFAS restriction status, firefighting foam consultation.
  • Environment and Climate Change Canada — PFAS Canadian Environmental Protection Act assessments, Prohibition of Certain Toxic Substances Regulations.
  • Australia DCCEEW — PFAS National Environmental Management Plan (NEMP), ADWG, ANZG water values.
  • Germany BMUV — PFAS TrinkwV (Drinking Water Ordinance) PFAS limits.

Scientific & technical references

  • ITRC PFAS Technical and Regulatory Guidance The engineer and scientist's reference document. Updated regularly; covers analytical methods, fate & transport, remediation technologies, vapor pathways.
  • ATSDR Toxicological Profile for Perfluoroalkyls Detailed toxicology, exposure pathways, health endpoints.
  • EPA PFAS Strategic Roadmap Federal framing of PFAS as a multi-decade priority across all media.
  • NACWA PFAS resources National Association of Clean Water Agencies. Biosolids RA modeling, public-utility perspective.

Legal & regulatory analysis (free / freemium)

  • JD Supra — PFAS topic page Aggregates law-firm client alerts, quarterly updates, and case analyses. Free with email registration.
  • Lexology — PFAS search Similar aggregator with stronger international coverage. Free with registration.
  • National Law Review Free, no registration. Strong on regulatory and litigation crossover pieces. Search "PFAS" for current coverage.
  • Federal Register Official daily journal of federal rulemaking. The tracker's Live FR Stream pulls PFAS-filtered content via the public API.
  • Environmental Law Institute (ELI) Independent research and policy analysis; produces periodic toxics-litigation reports.

InsideEPA (subscription)

  • InsideEPA PFAS News Daily trade-press coverage of federal PFAS policy, enforcement, and litigation. Integral has a companywide subscription — login credentials are at The Hive → Library Services → Company Subscriptions.
