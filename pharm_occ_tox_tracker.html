Monthly Cowork prompt

Purpose

Paste this prompt into Claude Cowork as a recurring monthly task. It searches for new publications in pharmaceutical occupational toxicology, OEL/HBEL science, TTC methodology, and extractables & leachables, then returns structured entries ready to upload to this tracker via Zotero CSV export.

Expected Zotero CSV columns for upload

When you receive results from Cowork, add them to Zotero and export as CSV with these columns: Title, Author, Publication Year, Publication Title, DOI, Abstract Note, Volume, Issue, Pages, Manual Tags

Prompt — copy and paste into Cowork

Monthly pharmaceutical occupational toxicology literature update — [Month Year] You are helping maintain a living literature tracker for pharmaceutical occupational toxicology and health-based exposure limit science. This field covers: OEL and HBEL derivation, permitted daily exposures (PDEs), acceptable daily exposures (ADEs), thresholds of toxicological concern (TTC), extractables and leachables (E&L) safety assessment, inhalation bioavailability of pharmaceuticals and biologics, genotoxic impurity limits (ICH M7), non-mutagenic impurity qualification, and cross-contamination control in pharmaceutical manufacturing. Search for peer-reviewed articles, guidance documents, and technical reports published in the PAST 30 DAYS. How to use the results

When Cowork returns results, add the papers to your Zotero library, tag them with the subject tags listed above using Zotero's "Manual Tags" field (semicolon-separated), then export as CSV and upload using the "Upload Zotero CSV" button. New entries will appear with a green "NEW" badge in the This Month tab.
